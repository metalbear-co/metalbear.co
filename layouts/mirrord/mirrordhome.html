Why developer teams choose mirrord
by MetalBear

Most solutions that connect local environments to the cloud rely on complex setups, like VPNs.

mirrord simplifies the process by working directly at the level of your local process. It overrides system calls and proxies them to the cloud, requiring minimal setup and no additional integrations.

  • Faster startup

    Up and running in 15 seconds or less.

  • More control over what runs where

    Choose which files, env variables, and requests stay local and which run in the cloud.

  • No root access required

    Works without extra permissions or system modifications.

  • Works with any cluster setup

    No need to reconfigure for service meshes, VPNs, or large-scale clusters (tested on 10,000+ pods).
